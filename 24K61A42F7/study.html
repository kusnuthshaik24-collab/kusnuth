Steps to Study Effectively

  1. Set a fixed time to start studying every day.
  2. Gather all books, notes, and stationery before sitting down.
  3. Turn off phone notifications to avoid distractions.
  4. Read the topic once to get an overview.
  5. Write down key points and important formulas.
  6. Solve practice problems or exercises.
  7. Review your notes after completing the topic.
  8. Take a short 10-minute break after every hour.
  9. Revise the day's topics before going to sleep.
  10. Repeat the process the next day with a new topic.
